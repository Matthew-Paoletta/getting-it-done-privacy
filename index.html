This extension does not collect, store, or share your personal data. It works entirely locally in your browser:

  • We capture a screenshot of your Webreg schedule only when you click the extension icon
  • Text is extracted from the screenshot locally to create an .ics file
  • Optional Google Calendar upload uses secure OAuth2 (Google's privacy policy applies)
  • No data is sent to external servers unless you choose Google Calendar upload
  • All processing happens in your browser - we don't see or store your schedule data
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time to remove all local data
